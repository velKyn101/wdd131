Budget Buddy - Site Plan

Budget Buddy - Budgeting Tool

This is your buddy to manage your budget.

An optional domain can be: budgetbuddy.com.br

The Budget Buddy will

Help you to manage your budget by allowing you to keep track of your expenses and income in an organized and intuitive way.

Buddget Buddy will be a tool to answer questions like:

How can I keep track of my expenses?

What is my total income?

How can I know if I have enough money in my budget?

How much will I be able to save monthly?

Budget Buddy Color Schema will be:

Primary: Navy Blue (#000080) - Headers and navigation.

Secondary: Light Blue (#66CCFF) - Buttons, CTAs and headlines.

Text: (#2e343b).
